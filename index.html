About Disconnected

Disconnected's mission is to stop Esports bullying and support those affected by online and in-person harassment.

Case Study

Players and team members in the gaming community often face aggressive behavior and verbal abuse, making esports bullying a prevalent issue. Unfortunately, despite the seriousness of the problem, esports bullying is often overlooked and not covered by mainstream media. This lack of attention not only worsens the issue but also makes it harder for players and teams to receive the support they need. Additionally, the lack of coverage can lead to a culture of silence, where victims of esports bullying are reluctant to come forward or speak out for fear of further harm or retribution. It is essential to raise awareness about esports bullying and to work towards creating a safe and inclusive environment for all players and team members, regardless of their skill level or status.
